About Us

Welcome to Grand Malabar

Malabar is a historical and geographical region located on the southwestern coast of India, specifically in the state of Kerala. We bring the vibrant flavors and rich culinary heritage of Malabar to your plate. We invite you on a culinary adventure that showcases the unique blend of spices, aromatic herbs, and fresh ingredients that define the Malabar region.

Experience our warm ambiance and let our attentive staff create a memorable dining journey. Whether you're a seasoned food lover or new to Malabari cuisine, we'll transport your taste buds to the enchanting flavors of Malabar with every bite.
